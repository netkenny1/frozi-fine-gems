Sizing & fit

Made to your size, EU 44–62, including half sizes. Resizing is free for the life of the piece. It is written into the ledger, not the receipt.

Care

Emerald is a garden, not a fortress: remove the ring for lifting and open water. Warm water, soft brush, nothing ultrasonic. We re-oil and re-polish at no charge, by appointment.

The stone's cutting house, export record, and date of entry into the ledger accompany the piece. Ask to read the file before you buy. We would rather you did.
