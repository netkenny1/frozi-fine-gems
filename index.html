The method

Drawn first, then made

I.

The drawing

Each commission starts on paper — graphite, then ink. The drawing is numbered, dated, and entered in the maison ledger before any metal is touched. If the drawing isn't right, the piece is never made.

II.

The setting

Stones are cut to the drawing, not the other way round. We work in Muzo-origin emeralds and recycled platinum only — two materials, known deeply, rather than a catalogue known thinly.

III.

The signature

Every finished piece is engraved with its plate number — the same reference written on its original drawing. Bring the piece back at any time; the ledger remembers it.
