Orders · Service · Privacy

Before a piece is made.

The important terms are agreed before the bench begins. This page describes the current process; the written invoice confirms the specification for an individual piece.

Ordering

Request, confirm, make

Bag
Adding a piece to the bag does not charge a card or reserve inventory. It prepares an invoice request for the atelier.
Confirmation
The atelier confirms the piece, size where relevant, price, and appointment or delivery arrangement before issuing an invoice.
Currency
Collection prices are published in UAE dirhams (AED).
Lead time
Pieces are made to order with a standard six-week bench lead time. Rush orders are not offered.
After the bench

Care for the life of it

Lifetime care
Resizing, re-oiling, and re-polishing are provided by appointment for Frozi pieces.
Provenance
The documented stone file described for the piece accompanies it, together with its original drawing.
Returns & exchanges
Because each piece is made to order, eligibility is discussed and confirmed in writing before the invoice is accepted. Ask the atelier about the terms for the specific piece.
Handover
Collection or delivery arrangements are confirmed individually with the order.
Privacy

Only what the appointment needs

The site has no advertising trackers, analytics trackers, or mailing list. Appointment details are used to reply, prepare the requested discussion, and coordinate the order or visit.

Do not send payment-card information through the appointment form. Questions about personal information can be sent to atelier@frozifinegems.com.
