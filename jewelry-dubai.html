Dubai · Private appointments

Fine jewelry in Dubai.

Frozi Fine Gems is a private fine-jewelry maison serving clients in Dubai by appointment. Every collection piece and commission begins as a drawing and is made at the bench in six weeks.

What is available

Collection or commission

The collection includes emerald and diamond rings, necklaces, earrings, and bracelets in recycled platinum. Prices are published in AED and each piece is made to order.

A private appointment can also begin a new commission, review an existing plate, establish ring or wrist size, or arrange lifetime care for a Frozi piece.

  1. 01 · WriteSend the atelier the piece, occasion, fitting, or commission you want to discuss.
  2. 02 · ConfirmThe atelier replies within two working days and shares the private Dubai appointment details.

Before visiting

Questions, answered

Where are appointments held?

Appointments are private and held in Dubai. Visit details are shared when an appointment is confirmed.

How long does a piece take?

The standard bench lead time is six weeks. The atelier does not offer rush orders.

Are prices in UAE dirhams?

Yes. Collection prices are published in AED. The atelier confirms the piece and its final specification before issuing an invoice.

Can I request a bespoke commission?

Yes. Appointments can cover a new commission, a collection piece, a fitting, or resizing.
